🗑️ Delete Your Account

App: Lal Vakhs

We respect your right to delete your account and associated data. You can request deletion using either method below.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

Open Lal Vakhs → Settings → Sign In section → Delete Account

This immediately deletes your cloud data and signs you out.

Option 2: Email Request

If you no longer have the app installed, send an email from the same email account you used to sign in to:

misrilibrary@gmail.com

Important: You must send this request from the email address associated with your Lal Vakhs account so we can verify your identity.

Send Deletion Request

What gets deleted

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • All Firestore data (tithis, favorites, chat history, preferences)
  • City and language settings

Timeline: In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are processed within 30 days.

Data not stored: We do not store date of birth or any payment information.
